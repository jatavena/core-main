### Testing / possible acceptance criteria

- Full backups (including files, images, and themes) are created automatically at scheduled intervals. (Test Case)

- Developers can integrate custom backup and recovery scripts into the backup process. (Test Case)

- Backup schedules and retention periods (e.g., how long backups are kept) can be customized via configuration. (Test Case)

- Restoring from a full backup successfully restores the entire PrestaShop environment. (Test Case)

- Logs or notifications confirm the success or failure of backup and restore operations. (Optional Test Case)
