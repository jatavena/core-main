# Stakeholder: Investors
**Stakeholder Image:**

![](https://openclipart.org/image/400px/126787)


**Stakeholder Name:**  
Investors

**Role:**  
Financial backers who provide funding for the product's development and growth.

**Responsibilities:**  
Monitor progress, ensure ROI, and influence strategic decisions to align with business goals.

**Interests:**  
Profitability, scalability, and long-term sustainability of the product.

**Influence:**  
High â€“ Their funding and strategic input significantly impact the project's direction.

**Communication Needs:**  
Investors need regular updates on project progress, financial performance, risks, and key milestones through reports, presentations, or periodic meetings.

**Expectations:**  
Investors expect a profitable and scalable product that generates strong ROI and aligns with market opportunities.

**Concerns:**  
Investors are concerned about delays, budget overruns, poor market fit, and risks that could impact profitability or reputation.

**Impact:**  
Provides the financial resources necessary for the product's development and ensures alignment with market opportunities.
