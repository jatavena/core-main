# Stakeholder: Product Owner
**Stakeholder Image:**

![](https://openclipart.org/image/400px/126787)


**Stakeholder Name:**  
Product Owner

**Role:**  
The Product Owner is responsible for maximizing the value delivered by the development team by prioritizing and managing the product backlog to align with customer and stakeholder needs

**Responsibilities:**  
The Product Owner is responsible for maximizing the value of the product by managing the product backlog, prioritizing features, and ensuring alignment with stakeholder needs and business goals.

**Interests:**  
Delivering a product that meets user needs, aligns with business goals, and maximizes value for all stakeholders

**Influence:**  
High â€“ The Product Owner directly influences the product's direction, priorities, and the value delivered by the development team

**Communication Needs:**  
The Product Owner requires regular communication with stakeholders to gather feedback and align priorities, as well as frequent collaboration with the development team through sprint planning, reviews, and daily stand-up.

**Expectations:**  
The Product Owner expects clear communication from stakeholders and the development team, timely delivery of prioritized features, and a product that aligns with the business vision and user needs.

**Concerns:**  
The Product Owner is concerned about unclear requirements, misalignment between stakeholder expectations and development priorities, delays in feature delivery, and the risk of the product failing to meet market demands or user needs.

**Impact:**  
Ensures the product aligns with the business vision, meets user needs, and delivers maximum value to stakeholders
