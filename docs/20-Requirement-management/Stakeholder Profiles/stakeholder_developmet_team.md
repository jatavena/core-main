# Stakeholder: Development Team
**Stakeholder Image:**

![](https://openclipart.org/image/400px/126787)


**Stakeholder Name:**  
Development Team

**Role:**  
Responsible for implementing, maintaining, and delivering the product based on the product backlog.

**Responsibilities:**  
Develops, tests, and delivers product features; ensures technical quality and adherence to best practices.

**Interests:**  
Clear requirements, achievable timelines, and a collaborative working environment.

**Influence:**  
High â€“ Directly impacts the quality and functionality of the product.

**Communication Needs:**  
The Product Owner requires regular updates from the development team on progress, challenges, and timelines, as well as clear and concise communication with stakeholders to gather feedback and align priorities.

**Expectations:**  
The Product Owner expects clear communication and collaboration with stakeholders and the development team, timely delivery of prioritized features, and a product that aligns with business goals and user needs while maximizing value.

**Concerns:**  
The Product Owner is concerned about unclear requirements, misalignment between stakeholder expectations and development priorities, delays in feature delivery, and the risk of the product failing to meet market demands or user needs.
**Impact:**  
Ensures the product is built to meet stakeholder expectations and technical standards.
