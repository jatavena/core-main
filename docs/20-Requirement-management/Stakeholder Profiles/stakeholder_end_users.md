# Stakeholder: End Users
**Stakeholder Image:**

![](https://openclipart.org/image/400px/126787)


**Stakeholder Name:**  
End Users

**Role:**  
Final consumers who interact with the product to fulfill their needs or solve problems.

**Responsibilities:**  
Provide feedback on usability and functionality through their interactions with the product.

**Interests:**  
A seamless, intuitive, and reliable product experience that meets their needs.

**Influence:**  
Medium â€“ Their feedback indirectly shapes product improvements and priorities.

**Communication Needs:**  
End Users need clear and accessible communication about product features, updates, and support options through user-friendly channels like in-app notifications, FAQs, or email.

**Expectations:**  
End Users expect a seamless, intuitive, and reliable product that meets their needs and provides value.

**Concerns:**  
End Users are concerned about poor usability, technical issues, lack of necessary features, and potential security vulnerabilities.

**Impact:**  
Drives the product's success by determining its adoption and satisfaction levels.
